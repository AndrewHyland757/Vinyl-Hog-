Shipping Information

Where do you ship to?

We ship to most countries worldwide from our warehouse in Dublin, Ireland.

How quickly can you process my order?

We aim to process every order on the day it is received. However, processing times may be longer during the Christmas period.

How much does delivery cost, and how long does it take to arrive?

We offer a flat delivery rate worldwide through FedEx, TNT, or DHL (All delivery methods include insurance and Track&Trace capabilities).

Orders under €50.00 - Delivery fee of €12.00

Orders over €50.00 - Free delivery

Ireland - Next day delivery

Europe - Approximately 3-4 business days

Worldwide - Approximately 5-10 business days
